Core Mechanics

GPS_TO_TERRITORY

Your physical movements draw a path on the map. Close the loop to capture the enclosed area and add it to your score for the current game session.

WEB3_POWERED_ECONOMY

Built on Stacks, the game features a transparent economy. Entry fees contribute to a smart contract-managed prize pool, and power-ups are purchased on-chain.

STRATEGIC_GAMEPLAY

Expand your captured area. Defend your trails from rivals and use power-ups strategically to control the largest territory by the end of the game.

System Architecture

Loopin is built on Stacks to power its game economy, secured by Bitcoin. This allows for transparent, smart contract-based entry fees, prize distribution, and in-game purchases, ensuring fairness and decentralization.
